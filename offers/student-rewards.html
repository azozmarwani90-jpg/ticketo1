Student Rewards

Claim your reward

  1. Create or sign in to your Ticketo account using your university email address.
  2. Check your inbox for the verification link and confirm your student status.
  3. Your unique code STUDENT50 will be added to your wallet within minutes.

Where you can use it

The STUDENT50 code is valid for one-time use on eligible festival and cinema tickets priced above SAR 120. It cannot be stacked with other promotions or applied to bundle bookings.
